Employee Benefits and Resource Guide
Fiscal Year: 2026
Human Resources Department

Welcome to our comprehensive team benefits guide. We believe in supporting our team members both inside and outside the workplace.

Health and Wellness:
The company offers 100% employer-covered premium health, dental, and vision insurance options for full-time employees and their dependents. Employees have access to mental health support networks, including up to 8 free therapy sessions per calendar year through our wellness partner.

Retirement and Financial Planning:
We offer a competitive 401(k) matching program. The company matches 100% of employee contributions up to the first 4% of their eligible salary, with immediate vesting. 

Remote Work and Home Office Stipend:
To ensure an ergonomic and productive working environment, remote and hybrid employees receive a one-time home office stipend of $500 to purchase equipment such as height-adjustable standing desks, ergonomic chairs, or high-fidelity peripheral devices. 

Paid Time Off:
Full-time employees accrue 20 days of Paid Time Off (PTO) annually, alongside 11 paid corporate holidays. We also offer a flexible mental health day policy where employees can take up to 3 unannounced rest days per year.
